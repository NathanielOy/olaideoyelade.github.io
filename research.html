📚 Core Research Methodologies

1. Generative Modeling in Molecular Discovery

Our lab develops customized transformer-based and diffusion architectures optimized to navigate chemical space safely. By exploring specific molecular properties, we train algorithms to generate functional therapeutics targeting specific protein pathways, actively validating model trust through defensive biosecurity engineering.

2. Digital Pathology & Multi-Modal Image Synthesis

In medical imaging, data scarcity is a recurring challenge. We design generative adversarial networks (GANs) capable of high-fidelity synthetic region-of-interest modeling. Our current frameworks automatically process multi-gigapixel clinical pathology files, identifying cellular characteristics to provide robust clinical decision assistance.

3. Bio-Inspired Metaheuristic Optimization

Deep learning pipelines depend heavily on hyperparameter tuning and optimal feature subset selection. We conduct intensive research into mathematical formulations of nature-inspired algorithms (such as the Ebola Optimization Method) to automate and optimize neural network structures dynamically, ensuring highly efficient convergence profiles.
