Director

Biomedical Image Analysis and Drug Discovery (BIADD) Lab

The BIADD Lab operates at the intersection of machine learning, oncology, and structural biology. Our mission is to accelerate therapeutic discovery and improve diagnostic precision through intelligent data engineering.

Core Research Areas:

  • 💊 Targeted Molecular Generation: Designing novel chemical structures optimizing binding affinity.
  • 🧬 Protein-Protein Interactions (PPI): Mapping macromolecular complexes to understand cellular pathways.
  • 🎗️ Oncology Image Modeling: Training GANs and CNNs for early breast cancer screening and microenvironment mapping.
  • ⚙️ Metaheuristic Optimization: Tuning deep architectures using evolutionary and bio-inspired search strategies.

Lead Investigator

Biosecurity and Generative AI Trust Lab

As molecular generative models become central to automated biochemical pipelines, safeguarding these systems against exploitation is paramount. Our biosecurity research evaluates the vulnerabilities, alignment, and structural integrity of deep generative models used in algorithmic drug design.

Defensive Focus Domains:

  • 🔍 Vulnerability Assessment: Identifying systemic blind spots and structural bugs within neural architecture pipelines for molecule discovery.
  • 🛑 Dual-Use Mitigation: Engineering architectural safeguards and guardrails to prevent AI models from generating restricted biochemical hazards.
  • 🛡️ Adversarial Robustness: Toughening deep generative networks against inputs designed to bypass alignment and verification parameters.
  • 📊 Model Governance: Developing metrics to audit model safety, training-data leakage, and downstream physical security.
